Messi (La Pulga)

Lionel Andrés "Leo" Messi is an Argentine professional footballer who plays as a forward for and captains both Major League Soccer club Inter Miami and the Argentina national team.

Neymar (The Prince)

Neymar da Silva Santos Júnior is a Brazilian professional footballer who plays as an attacking midfielder or forward for Santos. He is known for his dribbling, technical ability, playmaking, and finishing.

Luis Suarez

Luis Alberto Suárez Díaz is a Uruguayan professional footballer who plays as a striker for Inter Miami. He is regarded as one of the greatest strikers of his generation.
